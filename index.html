About Me

Hi! My name is Chanda Frey and I grew up in Arizona, and now currently live in Utah. My favorite people are my husband and our 7 children, daughter-in-law and 2 grandbaby boys. I tutor kids in reading and teach piano lessons. Some of my favorite treats are vanilla ice cream, cinnamon rolls, and dark chocolate. I like to get my mileage in by wandering the stores. Costco, Target, Home Depot, Lowes and Home Goods are my top choices. I will take a sunny day any day over a cold and blowy, snowy one. Too bad Utah doesn't have more sunny days. So as much as I identify as a flip-flop gal, you will find me in Uggs and wool socks as I try to weather through the majority of our seasons known as "cold".

Utah

Utah is one of the states on the western side of the United States. It looks a little like a boot. Utah has a range of climate from Below 0 Degrees in the winter in Logan, Utah to over 100 degrees in St. George, Utah. Utah has deserts, arches, salt flats, and snowy Rocky Mountains. Welcome to Utah where there is always something beautiful to appreciate!
